Kaspersky TXT to CSV Converter

Converts Kaspersky Password Manager's TXT export files into a CSV file that can be imported into other password managers and browsers.

Simply export your passwords from Kaspersky Password Manager, and upload the TXT file here.

No files are uploaded to any server; all conversion is done locally in your browser.
